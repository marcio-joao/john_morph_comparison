  Phenotypic similarity in insular disconnected populations of the endangered insular crab _Johngarthia lagostoma_

  Marcio João, Gustavo Mori, and Marcelo Pinheiro


  This respository cotains the data and scripts used to bridge morphometric comparisons between Brazilian island populations of the endangered land crab _Johngarthia   lagostoma_.

  The outcomes of this study are reported in the paper 'Isolation without divergence: comparing oceanic island populations of the land crab _Johngarthia lagostoma_'.   Data and R scripts are available in "Joao_etal_2026" folder.
